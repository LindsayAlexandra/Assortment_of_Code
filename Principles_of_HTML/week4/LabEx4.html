Keep in mind this quote from the writer Virginia DeBolt:

CSS allows effective techniques to make large areas of text on the Web more reader friendly. Readability and appearance are affected by line length, line height, white space, font choice and other formatting. CSS also is useful for grouping material into organized content blocks that can be given semantic and visual distinction to make the text easier to read and use.
