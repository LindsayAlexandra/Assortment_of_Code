Albums and Reviews

Ceremonials
Gothic, Celtic, bluesy, danceable rock: Sounds like a mess, yet quite the opposite -- due in part to Florence Welch's hurtling vocals, some of the most bewitching in both the rock and pop worlds right now. I found this a very enjoyably dark album
Lungs
Florence Welch's distinctive voice intertwines beautifully with harps, strings and drums as she sings her inimitable soul inspired indie and Tim Burton-style fairytales. A true Indie Rock Album.
Live at the Wiltern
Florence Welsh's music is a heavily orchestrated and dramatic mix of punk, pop and soul. Good quality for a live album.
