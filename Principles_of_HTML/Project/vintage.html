Project Proposal

My project is going to be a vintage clothing website. I my target audience is going to be people that are interested in vintage fashion or are involved in the sub-culture. I have found that a lot of the vintage clothing websites are not always the most appealing and very cluttered. My goal with this project in mind is to design and build a website that has a vintage feel, un cluttered and very appealing to the customer. I will be using different pictures of vintage fashion as well as implementing colors of the classic vintage style. I will have to do research on the vintage sub-culture and fashion to accomplish this project successfully.
