The Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and roof dome that can be opened.
How are yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furninshed with a queen size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experienc also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable wallking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
